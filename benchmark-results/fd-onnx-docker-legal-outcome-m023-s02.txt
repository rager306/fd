## Interpretation

This is packaged-environment quality evidence for the experimental ONNX 1024 Docker path. It confirms the local Docker image built in M022 did not regress the M018 legal quality result.

It does **not** mean ONNX should become the production/default runtime.

## Remaining blockers before production promotion

- external artifact provisioning/cache for hosted CI and deployments;
- full hosted CI ONNX image build after artifact provisioning exists;

- production default switch decision with rollback plan.
